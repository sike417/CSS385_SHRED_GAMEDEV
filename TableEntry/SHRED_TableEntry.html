SHRED

SHRED the mountains like never before! SHRED is a 2D snowboarding game for PC and MAC created with Unity 5.6. SHRED takes you to the top of the mountain and challenges you to get down without crashing. The more insane tricks that you land on the way the higher your score will be when you get down.
